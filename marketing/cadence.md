## Notes

- **HN timing**: Post between 9–11am ET on a weekday for best visibility. Avoid Mondays (crowded) — Tuesday or Wednesday is ideal.
- **Reddit spacing**: Never post to more than 2 subreddits in one day. Engage with comments for at least 24 hours before posting the next one.
- **Product Hunt**: Tuesday and Wednesday are the best launch days. Avoid Fridays and weekends.
- **Outreach pitches**: Include any traction numbers from weeks 1–3 (GitHub stars, HN points, Reddit upvotes). Social proof makes the pitch.
- **X threads**: Post in the morning (US timezones). Engage with replies for the first hour.
- **Adapt**: If HN hits the front page, accelerate the Reddit timeline. If it doesn't get traction, proceed as planned — Reddit stands on its own.
