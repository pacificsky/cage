## Strategy

Don't cold-DM on X. Instead:

1. **Engage first** — reply to their threads for a few days before mentioning cage
2. **Reply naturally** — when they post about AI coding agents, permissions, or sandboxing, reply with "I built something for this" + link
3. **Quote-tweet** your own cage posts and tag 1-2 relevant people (not all at once)
4. **Post your own threads** — share the story behind cage, tag relevant people sparingly

## Thread Ideas

1. "I gave an AI full shell access to my machine. Here's why I sleep fine at night." (hook → cage)
2. "The --dangerously-skip-permissions flag exists for a reason. But so does Docker." (practical)
3. "3 commands to sandbox any AI coding agent" (tutorial-style)
